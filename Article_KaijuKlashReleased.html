SOMETHING HAS AWAKENED! Kaiju Klash is Now Available on DriveThruRPG!

Published by Alien Fossil Arts

The earth is trembling, the sirens are blaring, and the ultimate battle for supremacy has officially begun! Alien Fossil is thrilled to announce that our brand-new tabletop roleplaying game, Kaiju Klash, is available right now. You can grab your copy and start your rampage today!

In Kaiju Klash, you take control of one of these titans, battling rival monsters while humanity struggles desperately to survive in the crossfire. True to the Alien Fossil design philosophy, we've stripped away the tedious math and heavy crunch to bring you a lightweight, easy-to-learn system that gets you right into the action. Battles are fast, destructive, and unpredictable, with military forces and collapsing buildings adding to the chaos. We wanted to create a game where the focus is entirely on cinematic storytelling and massive, city-leveling fun!

Here is what you can expect when you step into ground zero:

  • Fast & Easy to Learn: Some Kaiju Klash Character Sheets, some pencils and two 10-sided dice (d100) are all you need to get the destruction underway!
  • Spectacle Over Realism: The point is not realism, but spectacle! Make every hit, miss, and clash feel like part of the story the city will remember long after the dust settles.
  • Rewarding Chaos: Terror Points reward you for causing mayhem. Every strike, every toppled skyscraper, every scream of panic from below feeds your Kaiju's rising power.
  • Endless Customization: Using the creation tables, you'll roll (or choose) features for each part of your Kaiju's body, shaping its powers, weaknesses, and terrifying appearance.
  • Flexible Gameplay: Kaiju Klash can be played with a Game Master (GM) guiding the world, or in a GM-less mode where players share control of the setting and events.

Whether your monster rises from the ocean or crashes down from the skies, it’s time to smash, slam, and survive.
