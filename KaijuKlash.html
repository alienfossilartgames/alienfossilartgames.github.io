Kaiju Klash!

A Cinematic TTRPG of Towering Monsters, City-Shattering Combat, and Apex Dominance.

"Kaiju Klash! puts you in the driver’s seat of a colossal monster, battling other player-controlled beasts and completing your destructive objectives—all while fending off the best humanity can throw at you."

Build the Apex Beast

Forget small heroes and delicate systems. This is about raw, world-breaking power. You don't just pick a monster—you build it. Combine horrifying biological traits, devastating energy weapons, and unique mobility features to create a Kaiju tailored to your destructive desires.

Every session is a fast, cinematic clash where the rules facilitate high-octane action, ensuring the focus remains on smashing buildings, firing atomic breath, and wrestling your rivals into the dirt.

Unleash Maximum Destruction

Kaiju Klash is a simple but robust TTRPG that will have you clawing, stomping and chomping for more!

The Dual Conflict: Rivals & Resistance

A Kaiju's life is complicated. You must constantly balance the need to defeat your monstrous rivals—the only true contenders for the title of "Apex Beastie"—with the relentless interference of the military. Will you risk a valuable attack against a rival to wipe out a squadron of pesky jets, or let the humans distract them while you complete your objective?

The Gamemaster controls the Human Resistance Track, fielding increasingly powerful weapons of mass destruction as the players level the city. Be warned: the more you focus on each other, the stronger humanity becomes!

Victory: Destruction and Dominance

Victory is achieved not merely by eliminating the competition, but by establishing utter dominance. You win when you have completed your personal Warped Objective and are the last Functional Kaiju remaining on the map. Total destruction is guaranteed. Total victory requires ruthless strategy.
