Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 7, 2025

Welcome to Alien Fossil. We are committed to protecting your personal data and your right to privacy. If you have any questions or concerns about our policy, or our practices with regards to your personal information, please contact us at alienfosdilgames@gmail.com.

1. What Information Do We Collect?

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you express an interest in obtaining information about us or our products, when you participate in activities on the website, or otherwise when you contact us.

Information you provide:

  • Contact Data: Your name, email address, mailing address, and phone number (if required for orders).
  • Payment Data: Payment processing information (though we do not store full credit card numbers, which are processed by third-party payment providers like PayPal or Stripe).
  • Communication Data: Information you share when contacting us via email or using a contact form.

Information automatically collected:

We automatically collect certain information when you visit, use, or navigate the website. This information does not reveal your specific identity (like your name or contact information) but may include device and usage information, such as:

  • IP address and browser/device characteristics.
  • Operating system.
  • Referring URLs.
  • Information about how and when you use our site (pages visited, time spent).

2. How Do We Use Your Information?

We use your personal information for legitimate business interests, fulfilling our contract with you, compliance with our legal obligations, and/or your consent. We use the information we collect or receive for the following purposes:

  • **To fulfill and manage your orders:** To process payments, ship merchandise, and deliver digital goods related to purchases made on the site.
  • **To send you marketing and promotional communications:** If you opt into our mailing list, we may send you information about new games, news, or promotions. You can opt-out at any time.
  • **To improve our website:** We use data analytics (like Google Analytics) to understand how our users engage with the site, allowing us to improve user experience and content presentation.
  • **To respond to user inquiries:** We use contact data to respond to your direct questions or customer service requests.

3. Will Your Information Be Shared With Anyone?

We only share information with your consent, to comply with laws, to provide you with services, or to protect your rights. We may need to share your data with the following third parties:

  • **Payment Processors:** To handle payments for store purchases (e.g., Stripe, PayPal).
  • **Shipping & Fulfillment Providers:** To ship physical merch and games to you (e.g., USPS, FedEx).
  • **Data Analytics Providers:** To help us track and analyze website traffic (e.g., Google Analytics).

4. Do We Use Cookies and Other Tracking Technologies?

We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies (like web beacons and pixels) to access or store information. Specific information about how we use such technologies and how you can refuse certain cookies will be available in a separate **Cookie Policy**, which will be linked here.

5. How Can You Review, Update, or Delete the Data We Collect?

You have the right to request access to the personal information we collect from you, change that information, or delete it in some circumstances. To request to review, update, or delete your personal information, please contact us at alienfossilgames@gmail.com.
